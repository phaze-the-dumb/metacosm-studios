About Us
Founded in August 2021, Metacosm Studios is an indie, not-for-profit, virtual film studio that produces movies, short films, and episodic series shot entirely in VRChat.
Our Mission
We are a team of VRChat fans and volunteers driven by a common passion for VR filmmaking and the following core goals:

1) Produce cinematic VR film projects that we are proud of sharing

2) Create unique content that pushes the boundaries of VR filmmaking

3) Provide engaging work with equal opportunity for our volunteers
